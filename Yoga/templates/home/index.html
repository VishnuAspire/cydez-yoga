Yoga as a Way of Life

The primary goal of Yoga is to gain balance and control in one’s life; to free oneself from confusion and distress; to provide a sense of calm that comes from the practice of yogic asanas and pranayama or the breathing practices. Yoga improves posture, increases the intake of oxygen and enhances the functioning of all body systems, including respiratory, digestive, endocrine, reproductive and excretory systems. The ultimate goal of Yoga is self-realization.
